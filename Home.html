About Us  

Established in 2010, we are the tattoo company. We specialize in line and minimalistic tattoo designs. We offer both black and white and colored designs. We are located in the heart of Boston. We are pretty much accessible from all subways, buses as well as the commuter rail and are located amongst many restaurants, cafeterias, etc.

We have been a customer favorite for a very long time, especially because we're one of the first shops to ever offer minimalist designs exclusively. We use very safe tools and customer sanitation is our top priority.
