Equipment Used

Tattoo Gun

We use wireless tattoo machines to provide the best service possible. The chances of making a small error are almost negligible as there is no wire pull. We use, FK Irons Flux, Critical X Bishop Power Wands, Cheyenne Sol Nova Unlimited just to name a few. These machines also have a run time of almost 10 hours and hands can be used for an entire workday.

Nitrile Gloves

This may sound gross, but tattooing itself is the process of creating an open wound which becomes a tattoo. And hence cleanliness has to be at its peak and any bacteria, virusess etc. need to be removed. The first step other than having a clean workspace is the usage of gloves to maintain sanitation. While Latex Gloves, or a good and feasible option there are a lot of people with latex allergy and with in long-term use, can also cause allergy and other skin issues to the artist. To avoid this we use nitrile gloves, they give you the freedom to move and provide exact designs without compromising safety.

Inks

We do both black and white and color tattoos and so we make sure that we select the best ink for it to not fade for the longest time possible and so that our tattoos come out as vibrant as possible. We use brands like Eternal ink, World Famous ink, Silverback ink among many to make sure thw customer returns happy with the small piece of art on their body.

Numbing Cream

Pain is one of the biggest reasons why people are scared of getting tattoos, even if they really want one. Here at The Tattoo Co. we try our best to not let fear come in the way. We keep a number of creams handy for the same purpose Creams like Dr. Numb 5%, BCOT Pain Reducing Numbing Cream, TAONMEISU Numbing Cream are some common creams that can be found in our shelves.

Cleaning and Sanitation

Cleaning and sanitation are of the utmost importance to us. we strive to achieve the highest level of sanitation. Which can be comparative to that of hospital rooms. we put customer safety at the top and so ensuring our tools, spaces and personnel are claean has been and will always be our top priority. We make sure our artists wash hands thoroughly. We inspect for any cuts or open bruises to ensure their is no bacterial transmission. At any sign of discomfort to either the artisr or the customer. at the end of he day, all we want is our customers to go back with happy memories and a good experience.
